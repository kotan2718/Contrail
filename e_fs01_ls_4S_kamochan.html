The four seasons

KAMO-chan

photo kotan

From several years before, if winter comes, a wild seal appears in the seashore of Kamogawa.
Other seals which appeared in Tama River etc. did not appear next year.
I think that KAMO-chan( nickname of this wild seal ) is very pleased with this seashore.
It was reported by the newspaper that KAMO-chan appeared this year also.

First visiting. date: March 31 2004

Second visiting.
date: March 30 2005

This is the picture of December 28 2005.
He was swimming in the place of about 150m from the seashore.
The left of the picture is a sea gull and the right is a part of body of the seal.
Although I was not able to take a picture, I watched two seals.
He may have come here together with the bride.

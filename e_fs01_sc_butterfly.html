A certain person said, "Please make scenery of this island", and showed me a photograph.
I said to him, "Is there such an island truly?"

When I saw the photograph, I could hardly believe my own eyes.
The outline of the atoll looks like really a butterfly that expanded the wing.

I designed scenery of this island for FS2000 before.
However, an accurate place on the island cannot be specified to my regret,
therefor it has become an incomplete work at that time.

The true position of the island was understood by the mail
sent to me from Mr. I. S. several years later.
I wish to express my gratitude to Mr. I. S.

A fortunate person can see this island from a plane which goes from Australia to Japan.
And it is said that the person who was able to see this island can become fortunate.

Group D

Tauere

Tauere is a small atoll located in the northeast of Hao, and the distance from Hao is about 85km. The atoll has square shape. The belt of the atoll is wide, and has an oval-shaped lagoon on the inside. The lagoon is connected with the open sea in a shallow pass on the north side. It seems to be very deep though the lagoon is very small.

Photo Story 3

Microsoft Photo story 3 is a tool that converts the still pictures into animation.
When I was considering the way to burn an album of photos in DVD-Video, Mr. Kamui introduced me this excellent software.

When static pictures are built into a slide show and background music is added, it'll be a wonderful album. However, Photo Story 3 was surprising software to me, because I did not have the conception of converting the static photos into animation.

I made a sample short story with Photo Story 3.

It is wonderful software, > so I drew up the page which noted the impression of it and easy operating method.
A link to the home page of Photo Story 3 is included. Try it, if you have interest in Photo Story 3.

Unfortunately, Photo Story 3 cannot be used on Windows 10/11. However, I would like to archive this as a record of the existence of this interesting software.

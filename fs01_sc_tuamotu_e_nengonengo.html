Group E

NengoNengo

NengoNengo is located in approximately 100km southwest of Hao.
The atoll completely encloses the lagoon except a passage connecting with the open sea. The pass is located in the north side of the atoll.
In my impression, the plant of this atoll seems to be a little. The greater part of the land seems to be covered with sand.
There is a private airfield on the southeast of this atoll.

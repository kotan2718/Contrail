Group E

Akiaki

There are three flat-surfaced islands without the lagoon in Tuamotu islands. They are tereporepo, Nukutavake, and Akiaki. This small island located in the northwest 43km of Vahitahi. The atoll is covered by dense vegetation.

Size of Akiaki based on Rangiroa

There are not permanent residents though the atoll is covered by dense vegetation.

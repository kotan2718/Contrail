A mole and a dent...

It seems that the sun was busy this year.
The image is the transit of Venus, and a partial eclipse.
Solar photo was taken as follows.
Two polarizing filters are made to intersect at right angles.
And they were stopped with the Scotch tape to the camera.
That's all.

NOTE: This method has a possibility of breaking the CCD of your digital camera.
Don't perform this method if you don't know well the structure of a polarizing filter.

O.I.S.
